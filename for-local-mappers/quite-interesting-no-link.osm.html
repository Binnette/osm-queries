Interesting places without wikidata links

Run

Finds a range of potentially interesting, historic or noteworthy locations that don't have a Wikidata link.

Of course, what you might consider interesting, historic or noteworthy might be different. And lack of a wikidata link doesn't require that a) there is a wikidata entry or that b) it should have one.

But this might be a useful starting point to find locally significant places and either link them to wikidata, perhaps adding a new entry if useful.
