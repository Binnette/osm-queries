17 - Ways and their tags

Run

When querying for ways we can apply everything we've learned from querying for nodes.

That includes filtering them based on their tags. Or using them to search nearby on the map.

The query below selects just those ways in our bounding box that have been tagged as a tourist attraction. As the time of writing, that results in a single way which marks out the boundary of Uluru.

See the query comments for other variations to try, including finding highways, footpaths and amenities near to paths.
